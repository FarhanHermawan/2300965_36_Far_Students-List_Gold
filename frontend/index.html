Our Mission

The mission of Harvard University is to educate and inspire future leaders, advance knowledge, and address the world's greatest challenges through teaching, research, and public service.

About Harvard University

Harvard University, founded in 1636, stands as one of the oldest and most prestigious research institutions globally, situated in Cambridge, Massachusetts. Its diverse faculties and schools cover an extensive range of academic disciplines, producing influential alumni across various fields. The university's commitment to interdisciplinary collaboration and its iconic motto "Veritas" reflect its dedication to truth-seeking and innovation. With a rich history, groundbreaking research, and a globally recognized reputation, Harvard remains a beacon of academic excellence.
